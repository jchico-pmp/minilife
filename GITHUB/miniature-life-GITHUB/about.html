Who We Are

Miniature Life is the brand of tiny houses built in different countries. It is under a subsidiary of JC Homes, registered business as Miniature Life, Inc. The company had its humble beginnings in December 2016.

Our Founders

Miniature Life was founded by Joven Chico and Dr. Jekyll Hyde in the outskirts of Manila, Philippines in December 2016. Joven graduated as a civil engineer, worked as a software engineer and is currently the CEO of Miniature Life, Inc.

Our Mission

Miniature Life's mission is to transform the lives of people by simplifying their way of life. The company promotes work-life balance.

Our Goal

Our goal is to bring the brand in 20 countries in 2020. Long-term goal is be present in 100 countries by 2025.

Our Vision

We envision ourselves to be the leader in this so-called "tiny house movement". Miniature Life believes that even though families live in smaller spaces, they continue their happy lives the greatest they can be.

Our Corporate Social Responsibility

Miniature's Life is a member of the All Green Construction Committee of the United Nations, thus, the company uses more reyclable materials compared to the market. Moreover, Miniature Life employs hundreds to thousand of indigenous and local workers who make the community a better place.
